Era I

System in Service
of Purpose

For most of history, systems served purposes. Documentation supported the mission. Governance enabled the work. The machinery existed to advance the thing that mattered.

Era II

The System Outgrew
the Purpose

Systems built to serve the purpose gradually became systems the purpose had to serve. The tools got better. The ratio got worse.

Era III

Restoration

AI rebalances the relationship — absorbing system complexity so human attention returns to the mission. Not revolution. Restoration.
